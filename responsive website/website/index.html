Sexually transmitted diseases (STDs)

are infections that are passed from one person to another through sexual contact. Some STDs can be passed from a pregnant person to the baby, either during pregnancy or when giving birth. Other ways that STDs may be spread include during breastfeeding, through blood transfusions, or by sharing needles. There are more than 20 types of STDs. Here we will touch in detail only 4 of them: AIDS, Syphilis, Hepatitis B, and C. We will help you find out about the risks of having them and provide you with all the necessary information you may need.
