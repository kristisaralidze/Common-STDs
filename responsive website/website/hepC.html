Hepatitis C

affects approximately 296 million people, including over 6 million children under the age of 5. Hepatitis C contributes to an estimated 820,000 deaths every year. 25% of chronic hepatitis C infections progress to liver cancer.

Who is at risk for hepatitis C infection?

  • People with HIV infection
  • Current or former people who use injection drugs
  • People with selected medical conditions
  • Prior recipients of transfusions or organ transplants
  • People who received a transfusion of blood or an organ transplant
  • Health care, emergency medical, and public safety personnel after needle sticks, sharps, or mucosal exposures to HCV-positive blood
